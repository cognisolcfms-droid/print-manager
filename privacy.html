Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Welcome to CogniSol | CFMS (Cloud Functional Management System). Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we handle data within our application and hosted dashboard.

Core Privacy Principle: Your business data (Invoices, Customer Names, Revenue) is stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB. We do not upload your business data to our servers.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal identification information. The application functions as a local tool for your printing business.

2. Cookies and Third-Party Advertising

This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

3. Data Storage (IndexedDB)

All data created within the Print Store Manager (orders, services, and reports) is stored using your browser's local IndexedDB technology. This data remains on your physical device and is not accessible by CogniSol or any third party.

4. External Links

Our dashboard may contain links to our local Chrome Extension pages. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites or browser-specific data handling outside our domain.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us via our GitHub repository support page.
